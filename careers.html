job Description

* Client Service (30%)

– Serve as the lead point of contact for all client account management matt ers to build and maintain the good relationship with clients.

– Work closely with existing clients to gain insights on the needs of clients, market practices and brand awareness to recommend client to the right direction.

– Manage clients’ expectations and ensure satisfaction

* Project Development & Management (70%)

- Act as project leader for the medium and big client projects.

- Work closely with project teams in each step of planning and execution to guarantee project status and identify potential issues and/or opportunities within or related to the project.

- Ensure that the project tea m is making appropriate progress and output on a day-to-day basis.

- Follow up with client to proceed liquidation, report and payment for the project.

- Weekly report to Account Manager to ensure the smooth execution.

Requirements

– Number of years of experiences: 1+ years’ experience in account management. Solid branding and web/app design background is a plus.

– Level of language proficiency: Good verbal, presentation-style and written communication skills in both English & Vietnamese.

– Excellent in Microsoft Office (Excel, Word, Power Point).

– Soft skills/ competencies: Excellent organizational and time management skills.

– Good understanding about brand communication and project management.

– Result-oriented, friendly, warm, willing to learn with a positive attitude.

Benefit

– Salary: Attractive salary based on your ability and performance. Salary review twice a year.

– Social insurance and other compulsory welfare benefit as prescribed by VN law.

- Dynamic & friendly working environment.

- Exciting annual trip, company building, team building party, monthly team activities,...
